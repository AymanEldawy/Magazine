اللقاءات

اللقاء--- العنوان

KRSince 1968 Krah has been developing and producing Krah helical extrusion pipe production technology in the Westerwald region of Germany. The KRAH production technology is a helical extrusion (spiral wound) process that allows to produce profiled pipes for gravity application, solid wall pressure pipes and all kinds of special wall structures to produce fittings, manholes and accessories. Due to the advanced technology and a continuous development process, KRAH Pipes can be produced as a seamless, smooth and inspection-friendly inner surface together with an integrated socket spigot for electro fusion jointing and even for other types of jointing technologies that has provided great value to the global pipe market. The production procedure provides the possibility of customization and cost-efficient pipe design, especially for the required application, by the help designed wall structure.

اللقاء--- العنوان

KRAH Technology – KRAH, Germany manufactures the machine for the production of spirally wounded pipes for sewer or storm gravity as per EN 13476 standard and pressure applications as per DIN 16961. KRAH Pipes are 100% customizable – With our expertise and engineering skills, KRAH pipes can be designed for any kind of application and provide a unique pipe solution opportunity through the unique technology. Pipes are available up to ID 4000, with variable ring stiffness or pressure ratings. A great combination of flexibility enables to design economically and efficiently.
